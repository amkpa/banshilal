About Us

Banshilal Trusted Spices

Banshilal Masala is a brand of Indian spices that offers a wide range of spice blends and seasonings used in Indian cuisine. The brand is known for its high-quality ingredients and authentic taste.

Banshilal Masala offers a wide range of spice blends that are popular among consumers. Some of the popular spice blends offered by Banshilal Masala include Garam Masala, Pav Bhaji Masala, Chana Masala, Biryani Masala, and Chat Masala. These blends are made using a combination of various spices and herbs that give a unique flavor and aroma to different dishes. Banshilal Masala is known for its high-quality products and has been serving customers for many years.


Banshilal Masala are available in many grocery stores and supermarkets across India. You can check with your local grocery store or supermarket to see if they carry Banshilal Masala products. Additionally, Banshilal Masala has an official website where you can see their products online.


Banshilal Masala products are suitable for vegetarians and vegans. They do not use any animal-derived ingredients in their products, making them a great option for those following a vegetarian or vegan diet. Banshilal Masala specializes in producing high-quality spices and seasonings, and they take pride in using only the best ingredients to create their products. So, if you are a vegetarian or vegan looking for flavorful and authentic spices, Banshilal Masala is definitely worth checking out!


The shelf life of Banshilal Masala spices can vary depending on the specific spice and how it is stored. Generally, whole spices such as cumin seeds and cardamom pods can last up to 4 years when stored in a cool, dry place. Ground spices such as turmeric and chili powder have a shorter shelf life of around 2-3 years. It is important to store spices in airtight containers away from heat, moisture, and sunlight to extend their shelf life. It's always a good idea to check the expiration date on the packaging or label of the spice to ensure its freshness.
